3rd Quarter Challenge

 

As we enter into a new season, it’s time to clear our minds and go into Fall with a healthy mindset!

 

Mental health is how individuals think, act and cope with life and the stressors and challenges that are part of the human experience.

 

Individuals with good mental health wellness are better able to function during stressful situations. Good mental health wellness is reflected in several different methods:

 

1.     Bouncing back from adversity

2.     Communicating about your feelings

3.     Forming good interpersonal relationships

4.     Setting and achieving realistic goals

5.     Seeking help in difficult times

6.     Enjoying life to the fullest

7.     Self-appreciation

 

For this challenge, please review the 7 methods listed above and pick 3 that you either want to focus on or that have affected your life recently. Now, talk about it! With the challenge form emailed to you, state the method, the situation you found yourself in related to that method, what your typical reaction would be, and then what your new-found, good mental health reaction was.

 

The point of this challenge is to recognize habits of reacting to situations in a negative, non-productive way and instead, choosing to turn them into positive, learning experiences.

 

Examples:

Maybe you’ve realized when you argue with others, you get upset and walk away, never resolving issues. A new action could be expressing your feelings clearly and working with others to find a mutual agreement to the benefit of all involved.

 

Maybe you ran into some tough obstacles at work, making you feel overwhelmed. Your normal reaction might be to panic and give up, but your new action could be to get organized, ask for help, and start compartmentalizing your “to do” list to be more productive.  

 

Challenge starts Wednesday September 5th and ends Wednesday September 26th. Forms due Friday September 28th. All participants receive (1) QWA and will be entered into a raffle to win one of three Visa gift cards!

 

Use this as a tool to build better, healthier habits for the mind and hopefully learn something new about yourself.

Walking Club

 

Do you walk at lunch or on your breaks? Want to earn a QWA just for doing your normal walks? Join the Walking Club!

 

Walk for at least 25-30 minutes, 4 times a month (during your lunch or break) and earn 1 QWA each month for participating! To sign up, simply go to the front office and let them know that you would like to be added to the Walking Club sheet. Remember, it must be for a period of 25-30 minutes and you must walk at least 4 times a month. After you join, don’t forget to check out and check in with the front desk so they can log your times!
