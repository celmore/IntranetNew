Biometric Screenings are coming up!

Take an easy step towards a healthier you! Our upcoming biometric screening event will give you a chance to find out what your blood pressure, blood glucose, cholesterol, and BMI (body mass index) numbers are, and what they mean—free of charge. This screening gives you reliable information about your current and potential risk for preventable diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, and hypertension, so you and your health care provider can take steps toward preventing serious health problems in the future.

For current Wellness Program participants, the biometric screening is mandatory . If you are unable to make it to either of the on-site biometric screening events, you can see your doctor directly to complete a screening and then fax in the provider form. This form will be available in the ERC office and on the intranet site by next week (stay tuned).

We will have two people on-site who will be performing the screenings and explaining what the results mean to your health. The screeners will also give each participant a personal health report to take home. It is recommended to fast for 9-12 hours prior to testing to avoid misleading results, but it is not required.

Take a few minutes for yourself and your health. Register today for this event!

When: January 23rd & January 29th 6:00AM to 11:50AM Each Day, 10 minute increments

Where: 4th Floor SW Conference Room

How to register: Come to the ERC Office to sign up!

(The event is being administered by Vivacity – Premera’s wellness consulting company)

All participants will receive 1 QWA

Health Risk Assessment is now available through Premera’s website!

As some of you may know, each year we do a Health Risk Assessment Questionnaire as part of our Wellness Program requirements. These assessments help give you a picture of your overall health and allows you to see what you’re doing well and what areas may need some extra attention. Since we did this through Premera Blue Cross last year, most all of you should have a login with Premera to complete it again this year.

Just like the biometric screening, completion of the questionnaire is mandatory in order to remain on the Wellness Program. Failure to do so will result in removal from the Wellness Program and an increase in your insurance rates for the year.

In order to complete the questionnaire, you must login with your credentials you created last year. If you are new to the Premera plan, you must create a login on the Premera webpage, www.premera.com (there is a quick link on the Aero Controls Intranet page as well). Simply go to Premera.com, click ‘Log In’ on the upper right side of the screen, and select ‘Create new account now’.

Once your account is set up, please follow the instructions in the attached to complete the questionnaire.

You will want to set aside 15-20 minutes to complete the questionnaire. If you start it but cannot complete it until a later date, you may save and close the questionnaire window. It will pick up where you left off when you log back in. Premera will supply Aero Controls a report of all the employees that have completed the HRA, so screenshots or printouts are not required this year.

The Health Risk Assessment Questionnaire must be completed no later than Sunday March 31st, 2019. You will also receive (1) QWA for the 1st Quarter for completing the HRA.

If you have any questions at all, please come see us in the Employee Resource Center.

Walking Club

Do you walk at lunch or on your breaks? Want to earn a QWA just for doing your normal walks? Join the Walking Club!

Walk for at least 25-30 minutes, 4 times a month (during your lunch or break) and earn 1 QWA each month for participating! To sign up, simply go to the front office and let them know that you would like to be added to the Walking Club sheet. Remember, it must be for a period of 25-30 minutes and you must walk at least 4 times a month. After you join, don’t forget to check out and check in with the front desk so they can log your times!
